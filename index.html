"Encryption"

The definition of encryption depends largely on the context in which the word is used. While Gmail may use https, the contents of your messages are stored unencrypted on their servers.

  • Email contents can be encrypted using PGP. Most email services use SSL to encrypt your mail while it is being transferred to or from their servers, but this does not mean that the email contents are secure.
  • Chat can be encrypted with OTR or services like Cryptocat, among many other options.
